PIG DICE



    Rules

  1. Click the ROLL DICE button to roll and generate points.
  2. Click the HOLD button to collect the generated points this will switch to the other player's turn
  3. If you roll a 1, the rolled points are not collected and it becomes the other player's turn.
  4. If any of the players reach 100 points he/she becomes the winner of the game
