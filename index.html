PIG DICE


Rules

  1. Click the ROLL button to roll the die. Each roll is added to the total points.
  2. Click the HOLD button to collect the rolled points and then it becomes the other player's the turn.
  3. If a player rolls a 1 then the rolled points are not collected and it is then the other player's turn.
  4. First player to reach 100 points wins.
